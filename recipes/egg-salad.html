Simple Egg Salad

The simplest, good egg salad that is still delicious!

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs
  • ¼ cucumber
  • 3 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

How to Make

  1. Boil eggs
  2. Chop cooked eggs and cucumber
  3. Put chopped eggs and cucumber in a bowl
  4. Add ranch dressing, mustard salt and pepper
  5. Mix it all together

Voilà - now you have a bowl full of delicious egg salad. Enjoy!
